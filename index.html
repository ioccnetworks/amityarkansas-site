Welcome to Amity

Welcome to Amity, Arkansas!

Amity is located near the southern threshold of the beautiful Ouachita National Foreset in southwest Arkansas. Our small, friendly community is within close reach of many excellent attractions and locales, including:

  • De Gray Lake
  • Lake Ouachita
  • Lake Greeson
  • Hot Springs National Park
  • Crater of Diamonds State Park
